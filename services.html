Shrimp Farming

Shrimp farming, or prawn farming, is the cultivation of marine shrimp or prawns for human consumption. Our shrimp farming services emphasize ecological balance, efficient feed management, and disease control.

We prioritize water quality management and advanced aquaculture techniques to produce healthy and sustainable shrimp products for global markets.
